Alex Karev

Alex Karev, played by Justin Chambers, is another original cast member. Alex Karev is the "underdog" character of the storyline. At the beginning of the show, nobody thought he would be as successful as he ended up being. Alex Karev was a pediatric attending, Chief of Pediatrics, as well as Chief of Surgery at Grey Sloan Memorial hospital. Alex was part of the show for seasons 1-14. Alex Karev ended up developing into an extremely loved character by Grey's fans.

In the show, Alex became a fan favorite when Meridith's best friend, Christina, left the show. When Christina left, Alex took over and became Meridith's best friend. The bond between Alex and Meridith became something that was awed upon among fans.

Alex had many love interests in the show, however there were two main ones. The first one was a surgical fellow in his class. Her name was Izzie Stevens. Alex and Izzie got married because Izzie had cancer. Eventually, Izzie left Alex without any notice. Then, Alex met Jo. Jo was an intern when they met. They ended up getting married and were extremely happy with each other.

Alex's leave of the show left a lot of people questioning him. Alex ended up leaving for Izzie. He wrote a bunch of people in his life's letters explaining his departure, including Meridith and Jo. Alex up and left his whole life in Seattle to move to Kansas with Izzie Stevens. A lot of fans questioned this departure because this was so out of place for Alex. Justin is happily married to his wife, Keisha. They have 5 children together and one of them is even named Jackson. Now that Justin has left the show, he still continues to publicly support the ongoing seasons.
